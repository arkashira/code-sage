# Customer Journey Map for Code-Sage

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Tech professionals encounter challenges in code quality, debugging, and optimization.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about advanced code intelligence tools; existing tools are either too basic or not tailored for specific needs.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, and a sense of being overwhelmed by the complexity of code management.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Create engaging content (blogs, webinars) showcasing the benefits of code intelligence; offer free resources (e.g., eBooks, guides) on improving code quality.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and content downloads.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Tech professionals actively search for solutions to improve code quality and efficiency.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelmed by too many options; difficulty in comparing features and benefits of different tools.
- **User Emotions**: Skepticism, cautious optimism, and a desire for validation through peer reviews or case studies.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide detailed product comparisons, customer testimonials, and case studies that demonstrate effectiveness; offer a personalized demo or consultation.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and engagement with comparison content.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: Users decide to test Code-Sage to evaluate its capabilities.
- **Friction Points**: Complexity of onboarding; potential technical issues during initial setup; lack of immediate support.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with anxiety about the tool's performance and usability.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Streamlined onboarding process with guided tutorials; 24/7 support during the trial period; proactive check-ins from customer success teams.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate and user engagement metrics during the trial.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Users successfully integrate Code-Sage into their workflow and see initial positive results.
- **Friction Points**: Resistance to change from existing workflows; potential integration issues with other tools.
- **User Emotions**: Relief and satisfaction, but also apprehension about long-term commitment.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide ongoing training sessions, success stories, and community engagement; offer incentives for early adopters (e.g., discounts on subscriptions).
- **Metric**: Subscription conversion rate and user retention rate.

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Users experience significant improvements in code quality and efficiency, leading to consideration of additional features or team licenses.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about the value of additional features; budget constraints for expanding usage across teams.
- **User Emotions**: Enthusiasm for the product's capabilities, but concern about costs and ROI.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer tailored packages for teams, showcasing cost savings and efficiency gains; provide a clear roadmap for future features and updates.
- **Metric**: Upsell rate and customer lifetime value (CLV).
