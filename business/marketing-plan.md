# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
Code-Sage: The high-performance code intelligence platform that empowers tech professionals with advanced code analysis and actionable recommendations.

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**  
1. Code-Sage: Transform your coding experience with intelligent insights and recommendations tailored for tech professionals.
2. Code-Sage: Elevate your coding efficiency with our cutting-edge code intelligence platform designed for developers.
3. Code-Sage: Unlock the full potential of your code with advanced analysis and expert recommendations at your fingertips.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Alex the Software Engineer**  
   - **Daily Job:** Writes and reviews code, collaborates with team members, and integrates new technologies.  
   - **Budget:** $1,500/month for tools and software.  

2. **Persona 2: Jamie the Tech Lead**  
   - **Daily Job:** Oversees development projects, mentors junior developers, and ensures code quality and performance.  
   - **Budget:** $2,500/month for team tools and resources.  

3. **Persona 3: Taylor the DevOps Engineer**  
   - **Daily Job:** Manages CI/CD pipelines, monitors system performance, and automates deployment processes.  
   - **Budget:** $2,000/month for infrastructure and development tools.  

## Channels
1. **Content Marketing (Blog & SEO)**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $200 per customer acquired through organic traffic and content engagement.

2. **Webinars and Online Workshops**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $300 per customer acquired through educational sessions and demos.

3. **Paid Advertising (LinkedIn & Google Ads)**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $500 per customer acquired through targeted ads.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1:** Launch blog post introducing Code-Sage and its features.
- **Week 2:** Publish a case study showcasing early user success.
- **Week 3:** Release a webinar on "Maximizing Code Efficiency with Code-Sage."
- **Week 4:** Share a video tutorial on using Code-Sage for code analysis.
- **Week 5:** Publish an article on best practices for code intelligence tools.
- **Week 6:** Host a Q&A session on social media about Code-Sage.
- **Week 7:** Release a whitepaper on the future of code intelligence.
- **Week 8:** Share user testimonials and feedback.
- **Week 9:** Publish a comparison guide of Code-Sage against competitors.
- **Week 10:** Host a live demo of Code-Sage.
- **Week 11:** Release an infographic on code intelligence trends.
- **Week 12:** Recap blog post summarizing the first 90 days and user feedback.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features and prepare marketing materials.
- **D-0:** Official launch of Code-Sage with promotional campaigns across channels.
- **D+30:** Analyze initial user feedback and engagement metrics; adjust marketing strategies as needed.
- **D+90:** Evaluate product performance against success metrics; prepare for next phase of growth.

## Success Metrics
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Target 1,000 DAU by D+90.
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue ($MRR) Target:** Achieve $50,000 MRR by D+90.
